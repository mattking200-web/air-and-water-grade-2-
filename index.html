Air and Water in the Environment!

Grade 2 Science · Ontario Curriculum — Strand E

🌊 What Will We Learn?

In this unit we explore all 8 Ontario curriculum expectations about air and water! We learn how people affect air and water, how to save water at home, where fresh water is found around the world, what air and water are made of, where water comes from, the water cycle, the three states of water, and how all living things need air and water.
